ABOUT
ALFA BETA is a brand made just for you, the graphic designer. It is a simple, sleek, and modern, t-shirt clothing line for graphic designers that uses contrasting black and white or mesh for its visual impact and style. These pure cotton shirts with extended, short sleeves consist of over-sized, Gotham Bold typographic letters that interrupt the material space in asymmetrical ways. From various perspectives, the typography turns into abstracted geometric shapes, but when viewed from the correct angle, the typographic surprise soothes the graphic soul. So, adopt the designer lifestyle and live design.
